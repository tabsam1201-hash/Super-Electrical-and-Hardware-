# ⚡ Volt & Screw Co. — Store Manager

A professional, high-performance inventory, live billing, and smart AI photo-based price lookup application tailored for electrical and hardware retail businesses. Designed with a custom **Vibrant Palette** visual style built entirely on Jetpack Compose and Material 3 design principles.

---

## 📱 How to Install Directly on Your Mobile Device (No PC Required!)

Since you are managing your project on a mobile phone, there are two easy ways to get the installable `.apk` file directly on your device without using a laptop or computer:

### Option 1: Direct Download from AI Studio Settings (Fastest & Easiest)
1. Open the **Google AI Studio** interface on your mobile browser.
2. Open the **Settings menu** (usually the gear icon or sidebar dropdown in the top-right corner).
3. Tap on **Generate APK** (or *Export APK / Build APK*).
4. Wait for the cloud build to complete. Once finished, a direct download link for the `.apk` file will appear.
5. Tap the link to download the file directly to your phone, open it, and tap **Install**.

---

### Option 2: Automatic GitHub Actions Build
We have configured a fully automated pipeline via **GitHub Actions** in your repository. 
Every time you push changes or deploy your code to GitHub, our server automatically compiles the app for you!

#### How to download from your GitHub page:
1. Open your repository page on your mobile browser.
2. Tap on the **Actions** tab near the top of the page.
3. Tap on the most recent workflow run (usually named **Build Android APK**).
4. Scroll down to the very bottom of the run page to the **Artifacts** section.
5. Tap on **Volt-Screw-Co-App-Debug** to download the compiled ZIP file.
6. Open the downloaded ZIP file on your mobile phone, extract the `.apk` file, and install it.

> ℹ️ *Note: Since this is a custom-built app, your phone's Android OS might show a "Blocked by Play Protect" or "Unknown Sources" popup. Tap **Install Anyway** to complete the installation.*

## ✨ Key Features of Volt & Screw Co.

- **🎨 Vibrant Palette Design**: Powered by a luxury Material 3 royal purple, soft lavender, and premium charcoal visual aesthetic with responsive typography and spacious padding.
- **📸 Smart Camera Price Lookup**: Leverage built-in Gemini Vision AI to photograph any random electrical or hardware part (like an MCB switch, wire roll, or plier), automatically identify its category, standard retail price, and details, and match it with your local store inventory instantly.
- **🛒 Dynamic Invoice Billing**: Add items quickly from a searchable products list or live search inputs. Calculate precise Subtotal, standard GST tax rate (18%), and Grand Total automatically.
- **📋 PDF & Thermal Receipt Generator**: Generate beautiful printable, pixel-perfect digital sales invoices for customers on demand.
- **📦 Inventory & Low Stock Alerts**: Visually monitor exact product counts, SKUs, storage locations, unit-types, and receive color-coded yellow alert indicators for products dropping below 5 units.
